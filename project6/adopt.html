How to Adopt a Cat

Adopting a cat is a simple process with a big reward! Follow these steps to bring a cat into your life:

  1. Browse Our Cats: Check out the profiles of available cats and choose the one that feels like the right fit for you.
  2. Fill Out an Adoption Application: Complete our easy-to-use application form, and tell us about your home and lifestyle.
  3. Meet Your Cat: Once your application is approved, we'll arrange a meet-and-greet with the cat youâ€™re interested in.
  4. Finalize Adoption: If you and your new cat connect, we'll complete the adoption paperwork and make sure your new companion is ready to go home with you!

Adoption Fees

Our adoption fee is $120, which includes:

  • Comprehensive health check-up
  • Up-to-date vaccinations
  • Spaying or neutering
  • Microchipping for identification
  • A starter kit with food samples, a cozy blanket, and a toy

Support Us

We rely on the kindness of donors and volunteers to continue our mission of helping cats find loving homes. Here's how you can support us:

Donate: Every dollar makes a difference and goes directly to food, medical care, and shelter supplies for our cats.

Volunteer: Join us for events or assist in daily activities such as feeding, cleaning, and socializing with the cats.

Foster: Provide a temporary loving home for cats until they find their forever family. Fostering helps reduce overcrowding at the shelter and gives cats the love and care they deserve.

Interested in Fostering or Volunteering?

Fill out our Foster & Volunteer Application Form to get started. Share a little about yourself, your availability, and how you'd like to help. We'll get back to you as soon as possible to connect you with meaningful opportunities.
